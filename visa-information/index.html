A Business Visa is the ideal Visa type required for you to attend a conference. The details of the various types of Visas are available on The Republic of Zambia's Department of Migration

Business Visa Requirements

To apply for a Business Visa to enter the Republic of Zambia, applicants are REQUIRED to provide the following:
  1. Passport sized photo
  2. Passport copy of applicant
  3. Cover letter on company letterhead with full address and contact numbers addressed to the Director-General (you may wish to adapt the provided Cover Letter template)
  4. Invitation from host company (email: etd2024-info@unza.zm to request for an invitation letter after successfully registring)
  5. Certified copy of certificate of incorporation of host company (This is NOT necessary as The University of Zambia is a Public University of the Republic of Zambia established through the University Act of 1966)
