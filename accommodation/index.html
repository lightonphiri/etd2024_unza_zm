About Victoria Falls

Livingstone city is also the home of the Victoria Falls which is a breathtaking natural wonder renowned for its sheer power and beauty. Dubbed "The Smoke that Thunders," Victoria Falls is the largest curtain of falling water globally, stemming from the Zambezi River cascading over a 108-meter-high cliff. Visitors are treated to a sensory delight, especially during peak flow season, with mist rising high into the sky, forming mesmerizing rainbows. Adventure seekers can indulge in activities like bungee jumping, zip-lining, and helicopter rides, while those seeking tranquillity can enjoy sunset cruises along the Zambezi River. Whether you're drawn by nature, adventure, or culture, Victoria Falls promises an unforgettable journey, showcasing the beauty of the natural world.
