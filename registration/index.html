Cancellations

Please note that all cancellation refunds are will ONLY to be processed after the end of the ETD 2024 event. Refunds will be issued back to the original account used to make registration payments. In case of cancelations please contact the Local Conference Organising Committee at: etd2024-info@unza.zm

Cancellation thresholds

  • 100% refund during early registration period
  • 50% refund during regular registration period will be eligible for a 50% refund
  • 0% refund during or after the late registration period (except in cases of visa denial)
